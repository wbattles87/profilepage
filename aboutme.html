Will Battles All About Me

Born in Florida, just outside of Miami, I moved here when I was 12. After High School, I went to Texas A&M at Galveston and got my degree in Maritime Business administration. I graduated in 2009 and initially got my start as a customer service Rep for a container line. After a year, I got my second job as an Assistant Operations Manager for a terminal operating company


I felt as the days went that this just wasn't what I wanted to do with my life and decided to make a change. I enrolled for summer class at UHCL and finished my Computer Engineering degree from there in 2017. I learned a lot but didnt feel I was equipped enough to enter the technology work force so I enrolled in the UT Coding bootcamp in September of 2017 and just finish it in March of 2018. With all these new skills at my disposal, I feel that I am ready for my first start as a programmer.


In my off time, I love to do a lot of different things. I enjoy tinkering around with my large saltwater aquarium and watching my corals and fish grow. Going to Texan, Astro and Rockets game are also always on the table for me and my other half(especially lately with the Astros title and how well the rockets are doing!). Of course, coding is fun and I try to learn something new every day.
